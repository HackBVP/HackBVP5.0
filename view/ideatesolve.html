Ideate 'N' Solve

The second part of the triathlon will witness the participation of folks who wish to do solve problems pertaining to the UN's 17 SDGs. This event will ensure teams have a clear perspective on the problem they are trying to solve, the solution they're coming up with, it's need in the market, business prospect and utilisation of Google technologies.

Register Here

Rules 🔧

  • There will be 2 to 4 people in one team.
  • The theme for the event will be the UN's 17 Sustainable Development Goals.
  • Deadline to Register: 27th February 2022
  • An email with the entire structure of the ideathon will be sent to the registered teams by 28th February 2022
  • There will be a total of 2 rounds for the participants.
  • The first round will serve as an elimination round post which Top 10 teams will be selected.
  • During the second round, teams will have to present their ideas live in front of our judging panel and respond to interjections.
  • The judging criteria will be creativity, novelty, features, ease of implementation, practicality, usage of Google technologies in the technical stack and its contribution and relevance to UN's 17 SDGs.

Theme 🌟

The Sustainable Development Goals or Global Goals are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all". The SDGs were set up in 2015 by the United Nations General Assembly and are intended to be achieved by the year 2030.
