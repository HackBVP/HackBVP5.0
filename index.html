Welcome to HackBVP 5.0

Hack@Bvp 5.0 is a 24 - hour offline hackathon.
The primary objective of this hackathon is to foster data - driven innovation between the wider technology community. Teams of developers, designers and innovators will come together to build prototype of fully functional applications that tackle issues in our enlisted themes

Our Mission

Through this hackathon, we aim at creating a platform that advocates changes and contributes towards building relevant solutions in order to create a more sustainable world.

Our Vision

Our mission for this 24 hour hackathon is to provide the participants with ample exposure regarding the problems that they might face when they start working. Judging Criteria


Theme

How much the solution is aligned with mentioned theme.

Uniqueness/ Originality

Whether the idea has been thought already by someone.

Feasability

The functional and technical feasibility of the solution.

Cost of Implementation

How much cost would it need (cost-benefit analysis) with its feasibility.

Effectiveness of Idea

How much it would effect the world.

Implementation completeness

How much of it you have successfully implemented and demonstrated.
